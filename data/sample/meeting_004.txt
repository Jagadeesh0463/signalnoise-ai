Incident Review — LMS Platform Outage
Date: Q3 Week 2
Incident Owner: Vikram Nair
Attendees: Meena Pillai, Arjun Desai, Suresh Babu

Summary of incident: LMS platform experienced a full outage for 4 hours on Tuesday. 1,200 learners were affected during a live cohort session. Root cause was an unannounced database schema migration pushed to production without a rollback plan.

Vikram confirmed the change was pushed by a contractor who bypassed the change management process. Meena noted this is the third deployment incident in six weeks. Arjun raised concern that the team lacks a proper staging environment — all testing is done directly in production.

Suresh escalated that monitoring alerts were suppressed during a maintenance window and were never re-enabled. The outage went undetected for 47 minutes. SRE escalation was only triggered when learners began reporting issues on the support channel.

Risk assessment: High risk of repeat incident. No staging environment. Monitoring gap unresolved. Change management process not enforced for contractors.

Immediate actions:
- Vikram to implement contractor access controls by end of week
- Meena to restore monitoring alerts before next cohort session
- Arjun to draft staging environment proposal for director approval
- Suresh to document rollback procedure in runbook
